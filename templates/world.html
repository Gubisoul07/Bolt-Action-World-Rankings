How ratings are calculated

Iterative SoS (default): r = (W + 0.5·D) / games. SoS = mean of opponents' adjusted rate, iterated to convergence. Rating = 1000 + 1000·(r + SoS_adjustment − 0.5).

One-deep SoS: same formula but SoS uses raw rates (single pass).

Sequential Elo: games processed in chronological order (year → round), K={{ 32 }}. SoS not shown.

Only events with full round-by-round pairings are rated. Summary-only events appear in the historical record on the home page.
